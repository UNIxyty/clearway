Briefing/consultation provided

komentovány osobně synoptikem. Dokumentace pro předletovou přípravu a

Information for domestic flights is available continuously. Within hours of operation 
synoptic specialist provides the commentary personally. Pre-flight briefing and 
domestic flight documentation is provided up to 30 minutes after request, for 
international flights up to 2 hours after request. Commentary is given in Czech or 
English language.
