VRDA AD 2.22    FLIGHT PROCEDURES 
1. VFR operations within the vicinity of Maafaru  
 
VFR aircraft operating within Maafaru CTR shall contact Maafaru Tower frequency 118.6. When 
Maafaru Tower is closed, pilots may continue to monitor and transmit position information on 
Maafaru Tower frequency. VFR aircraft departing from resorts within Maafaru  CTR outside 7NM 
from Maafaru can climb up to 500 feet without ATC clearance after transmitting their intention to 
depart on Maafaru Tower frequency 118.6.
