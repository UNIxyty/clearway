movimentazione al suolo.

rilevato dai trasmissometri disponibili.

With RVR/visibility lower than 550m and equal or greater than 400m
taxiing is allowed onlly with follow-me assistance.
The RVR reference for taxiing operations is the minimum value
detected by the available transmissometers.

○ se non riceve tali istruzioni tramite segnali luminosi si ferma in
pista, in corrispondenza della TWY C in attesa del follow- me per
raggiungere lo stand di parcheggio.
Arriving aircraft:
• If, when the failure occurs, taxiing instructions has been already
received, continue rigorously on the assigned route, up to the
clearance limit received and wait for the follow-me for parking or, if
instructed to reach it, up to the stand;
 
• if the failure occurs in flight or in any case no taxiing instructions
has been received:
○ if specific instructions, via light signals are provided, clear the
landing area and wait for the follow-me to reach the parking stand;
 
○ if such instructions via light signals are not provided stop on the
runway, abeam TWY C, and wait for the follow-me to reach the
parking stand.
