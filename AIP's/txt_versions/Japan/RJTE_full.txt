3. Missed Approach Procedure for PAR/ASR Approach
Unless otherwise instructed by ATC, execute each missed approach procedure as follows.
(1) PAR RWY 09: AT guidance limit, climb to 500FT on HDG093°, turn left, to 2400FT via TET R340 to LULKU and hold. 
(2) ASR RWY 09: AT guidance limit, turn left, climb to 2400FT via TET R340 to LULKU and hold. 
(3) ASR RWY 27: AT guidance limit, turn right, climb to 2400FT via TET R340 to LULKU and hold.

4. Lost communication procedures for arrival aircraft under radar navigational guidance
If radio communications with Tateyama GCA are lost for 1 minute in the pattern, or 5 seconds (PAR)/15 seconds
(ASR) on final approach, squawk Mode A/3 Code 7600 and ;
(I) 1. Contact Tateyama Tower.
2. If unable, proceed in accordance with Visual Flight Rules.
3. If unable,proceed to SAPIM at last assigned altitude or  2500 ft whichever is higher and execute TACAN A
approach.
(II) Procedures other than above will  be issued when situation required.
